Production Assistant (Female Only)

Job ID 2500033

Job Description

We are seeking a skilled and motivated individual to join our production team to ensure the highest standards in creating our products.

Responsibilities

  • Operating machines and tools in the butter production line.
  • Accurately and safely packaging the butters in designated boxes.
  • Coordinating with the packaging team for inventory management.
  • Disinfecting the workstation at the beginning and end of each shift.

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of machinery operation (training will be provided).
  • Strong attention to detail to ensure recipe and weight accuracy.

Benefits

  • BDT 10,000 Salary
  • 1 annual festival bonus
